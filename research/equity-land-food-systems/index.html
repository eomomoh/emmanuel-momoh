At the Equity, Land, and Food System Lab (UC Davis), I worked on projects examining food networks and urban planning for agriculture in California. My role combined data science, spatial analysis, and leadership in collaborative, policy-relevant research.

Duties

  • Led data collection on web-scraping publicly available data on California Alternative Food Networks.
  • Coordinated weekly meetings and ensured project objectives were met.
  • Cleaned and prepared the dataset alongside American Community Survey data, analyzed with QGIS and Python. Two manuscripts are in preparation from this analysis.
  • Directed a team to collect and evaluate 428 California general plans from a novel California general plan database to understand how cities plan for food & agriculture.
  • Contributed to one published manuscript in the Journal of Planning Education and Research, with another currently in preparation.
